Manage channel settings

You can manage your channel settings in youTube Studio. change everything from your country / region to your channel's visiblity.

  1. Sign in to YouTube Studio .
  2. From the left menu, select Setting .
  3. From the left menu, select Channel .
  4. Set the channel setting and select Save .
